Jollof

Description

Whet your appetite with one of the most celebrated dishes in the World!

A simple meal made with vegetables, beef and stew mixed with rice, a pint of salt and just the right amount of water.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Tomato puree
  • Spices
  • Beef
  • Carrots
  • Green Pepper
  • Curry powder & Nutmeg
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Pour oil into a sauce pan
  2. Add sliced onions
  3. Add blended pepper, onions and fresh tomatoes
  4. Add tomato puree
  5. Mix with spices of your choice
  6. Pour beef into the stew
  7. Add one and a half cup of water
  8. Add Rice, nutmeg and curry powder
  9. When almost ready, add diced carrots and green pepper to garnish
